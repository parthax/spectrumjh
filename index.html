Words from Head of the Department


Physics, which also means ‘knowledge of nature’, is a subject of science which studies matter, its movements and its action through space and time in nature. The first and main important objective of physics is to understand how the universe functions and assist in making new discoveries with time. Physics is one of the most important subjects and areas of study which fuels most of the work we get done by today's machines.
